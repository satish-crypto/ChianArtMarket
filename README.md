# ChainArt Market

## Project Description
ChainArt Market is a decentralized, Ethereum-based marketplace for digital art NFTs. Artists can mint unique digital artworks, set custom prices, and sell peer-to-peer, while users can discover, purchase, and own digital art in a trustless, transparent manner.

## Project Vision
To empower creators around the world with direct, trustless access to a global digital art economy, eliminating intermediaries and enabling equal opportunities for artists to monetize their talent.

## Key Features
- Mint unique digital artworks as NFTs, each backed by a metadata URI.
- List digital artworks for sale or unlist them at any time.
- Purchase artworks directly from creators or current owners, with instant ownership transfer.
- No central authority; all transactions are on-chain and transparent.
- Easy retrieval of all owned NFTs via a simple function.

## Future Scope
- Support for royalties, allowing creators to earn on secondary sales.
- Integration with on-chain metadata standards (e.g., ERC721).
- Enhanced search and filtering of artworks.
- Social features such as artist profiles and follower systems.
- Multi-chain support for broader reach and lower fees.
